Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Choose how you would like to delete your UniNest account and associated data.

Delete Instantly via the App (Recommended)

For the fastest security and instant account termination, we recommend deleting your account directly inside the UniNest mobile application:

1

Open the App

Launch the UniNest app on your mobile device and log in.

2

Go to Profile

Tap on your profile picture in the navigation bar to open your Profile Page.

3

Access Privacy Settings

Tap on the Settings gear icon, and select 'Privacy & Data' or 'Privacy Settings'.

4

Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom, tap 'Delete Account', and confirm the security warning. All data is deleted instantly.

Submit Deletion Request

If you have uninstalled the app and need to delete your account, submit this request. We will process your deletion request within 48 hours.

Warning: Data Loss is Permanent
  • Your user profile, display name, and avatar will be permanently removed.
  • Your active schedules and timetables will be permanently deleted.
  • All messages, chats, and shared group files will be destroyed.
  • Location logs, friend lists, and active SOS/check-ins will be cleared.
