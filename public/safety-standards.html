Safety Standards Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

At UniNest, the safety and well-being of our university community is our absolute highest priority. UniNest is designed as a platform for verified university students aged 18 and older. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of child exploitation, abuse, or the distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy Against CSAE

UniNest strictly prohibits any content or behavior that sexually exploits or endangers children. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • The sharing, distribution, or promotion of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Grooming behaviors or any attempts to solicit sexual interactions with minors.
  • The sexualization of minors in any form, including text, images, or links.
  • Content that encourages or promotes the sexual exploitation of minors.

2. User Reporting & Moderation

UniNest empowers its community to maintain a safe environment through robust in-app reporting tools:

  • In-App Reporting: Users can flag and report any suspicious, inappropriate, or illegal content directly within the app via the "Report" button available on all posts, messages, and user profiles.
  • Immediate Review: Our moderation team investigates all reports regarding child safety and CSAE with the highest priority.
  • Content Removal: Any content identified as CSAM or a violation of our child safety standards will be immediately removed from the platform.
  • Account Termination: Accounts found violating these safety standards will be permanently banned without prior notice.

3. Law Enforcement Cooperation

UniNest is fully committed to complying with all regional, national, and international child safety laws. When we become aware of content or behavior that involves the sexual exploitation of a minor, we will:

  • Preserve the relevant data and evidence.
  • Immediately report the incident and the offending user to the appropriate national authorities and law enforcement agencies (such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children - NCMEC).
  • Fully cooperate with law enforcement investigations regarding CSAM and child endangerment.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to report a severe violation of our child safety standards, please contact our designated safety representative immediately.
