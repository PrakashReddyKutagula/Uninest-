Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I import my university timetable?

Go to the Timetable tab and tap the '+' or 'Upload' button. You can upload the official PDF provided by your university (e.g., FFCS, DSU, Alliance, Gitam). UniNest will automatically parse the document and build your schedule.

My timetable isn't parsing correctly. What should I do?

If your university's PDF format recently changed or isn't fully supported, please email your timetable PDF to our support email with the subject "Timetable Parsing Issue". We will update our parsers to support your format!

How do I report a bug or inappropriate content?

To report a bug, email us with screenshots and a description of the issue. To report inappropriate content or abusive users, use the Report button located on the top right corner of any user's profile, post, or message inside the app.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account directly inside the app. Go to the Profile tab, tap the Settings (gear) icon, scroll to the bottom, and select Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data, posts, and timetables from our servers.
